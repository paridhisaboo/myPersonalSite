COAT

A co-op tracker that reads my inbox so I don’t.

COAT links Gmail and Outlook, and a model I trained on ~1,200 self-labelled emails sorts each into offer, interview, or rejection — then drops interviews on your calendar. A regex fallback catches anything low-confidence.

03

SignSpeak

Indian Sign Language → speech, in real time.

SignSpeak watches a live camera feed and speaks what it sees. I fine-tuned a MediaPipe hand-landmark model on a self-curated 26-letter dataset. The hard part isn’t one frame — it’s knowing when a letter ends and a word begins.

  • a 10-frame majority vote kills jitter mid-gesture
  • a thread reads 1.5s pauses as word breaks, then fires TTS

04

Netflix Ratings

IMDb + Rotten Tomatoes, right on the tile.

A Manifest V3 Chrome extension that stamps live IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes scores onto Netflix cards before you hover. Netflix is an SPA that swaps its DOM constantly, so the trick was a MutationObserver that re-injects on every navigation — no reloads, no flicker.

  • an OMDb background service worker fetches + caches scores
  • MutationObserver survives SPA route changes
