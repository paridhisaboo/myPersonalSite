About

Curious about everything.

I’m Paridhi — a CS student at Waterloo, but the code is only half of it. My real hobby is perception: chasing new tastes, reading texture through my fingertips, and looking hard at art. I’m interested in how things feel before I can explain why.

Looking

01

I love work that turns the artist into the subject. Cindy Sherman’s Untitled Film Stills — where she plays every character in a movie that never existed — basically wrote the language of self-portraiture that so many women photographers speak now.

On the softer, stranger end: Aya Takano’s dreamy cosmic figures and the emotional shorthand of shōjo manga. I collect the way images make me feel more than the way they’re composed.

  • Cindy Sherman
  • Aya Takano
  • Shōjo manga
  • Self-portraiture
