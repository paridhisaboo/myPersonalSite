About

Curious about everything.

I’m Paridhi — a CS student at Waterloo, but the code is only half of it. My real hobby is perception: chasing new tastes, reading texture through my fingertips, and losing hours in a good playlist or a good book. I’m interested in how things feel before I can explain why.
